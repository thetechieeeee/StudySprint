# studysprint
🎮 A gamified study planner that helps students track and complete their syllabus like a game

⚡ StudySprint — Make Your Study Plan Feel Like a Game
StudySprint is a fun, gamified study tracker that turns your syllabus into mini-missions. Whether you're preparing for exams or just trying to stay organized, this simple tool helps you stay motivated and make real progress — one topic at a time.

✨ What It Does
Paste your syllabus and convert it into a list of “missions”

Track your progress with three clear states: Not Started, In Progress, Completed

See total topics and how many you’ve finished

Get new motivational quotes every time you refresh

Clean, distraction-free interface — no login, no backend

💭 Why I Built This
I’ve seen many students struggle to complete their syllabus — and honestly, I’ve felt the same. Sometimes, even looking at the syllabus feels overwhelming and boring. That’s why I created StudySprint — a simple web app that makes studying feel like a game. It’s more visual, more interactive, and definitely more motivating.

👩‍💻 Who It’s For
Students who want to study smarter, not harder

Anyone preparing for exams or managing large topics

People who love a visual, game-like approach to productivity

🛠 Tech Stack
HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla)

100% front-end — lightweight and fast

💡 Bonus Tip
Have a photo or handwritten syllabus?
Just upload it to ChatGPT and ask:

“Extract the topics from this image as a list” 📸 ➡ 📄
Then paste that list into StudySprint and start your journey!
